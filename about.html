About Me

I am Oosman Khwaja and I am a Multimedia Design and Development student at Humber Polytechnic. I have an interest in becoming a UX/UI Designer and learning more about the profession. As time goes on, I have gotten better at creating user experiences and utilizing Figma in order to create that experience.

My journey into this field began when I started noticing how certain apps and websites felt more intuitive and enjoyable to use than others. This curiosity led me to explore the world of design, where I quickly realized the importance of user experience in shaping digital products. As I deepened my understanding, I became fascinated by how small design choices could significantly impact user interactions. Over time, I developed my skills in wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing, with Figma becoming my go-to tool for bringing ideas to life.

Beyond technical skills, my approach to design is rooted in empathy—ensuring that every interface is accessible, visually appealing, and easy to navigate. I strive to create experiences that not only look good but also feel seamless and intuitive. As I continue to grow in this field, I aim to collaborate with diverse teams, learn from industry professionals, and contribute to projects that make a meaningful impact. My goal is to design digital experiences that enhance people’s daily lives while constantly pushing myself to improve and innovate.
